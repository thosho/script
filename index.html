About Fountain Script Pro

This tool auto-formats your text into a screenplay. It's designed to be fast, offline-first, and mobile-friendly.

Auto-Formatting Rules

  • Scene Headings: Type `int` or `ext` and the line will be capitalized. A `.` is added automatically.
  • Character: A name in ALL CAPS on its own line.
  • Transition: A line ending in `TO:` or starting with `FADE`.
